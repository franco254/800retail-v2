Who We Are

The origin of our journey

Al Ameen Group is the largest fixture manufacturing company in the Middle East. We are committed to delivering functional, innovative, and revolutionary solutions — with a culture of value woven through every project.

800 Retail Solutions is our manufacturing brand — specialising in custom-designed retail furniture, displays and consumer goods. We collaborate closely with clients to deliver the highest-quality products, supported by our in-house design team, technicians and cutting-edge equipment.

We proudly bear the "Made in UAE" unified national mark — a symbol of formality and trust for locally manufactured solutions, recognised across 25+ countries.

Production Methodology

The 5S framework

Every department operates under the Japanese 5S lean manufacturing system — the foundation of our consistent, defect-free production quality.

S
Sort

Remove unnecessary items. Every tool and material has a defined purpose.

S
Set in Order

Arrange necessary items in the most efficient order. Everything in its place.

S
Shine

Clean and inspect daily. Defects spotted before they become problems.

S
Standardise

Create standards for the first three S's. Consistency across all 12 departments.

S
Sustain

Maintain and review standards. Continuous improvement built into culture.
